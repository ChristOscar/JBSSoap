The Soul of the Soap

A journey from a family kitchen to a wellness-focused studio, driven by the radical idea that simplicity is the ultimate luxury.

Born of a Mother’s Love

JBS Soaps & Co. wasn't born in a boardroom; it began with a mother's frustration. Our founder, Shana Adams, started looking at the ingredient labels of her family's skincare and realized she couldn't pronounce half of what was touching her children's skin.

She believed that what we put on our bodies is just as vital as what we put in them. This sparked a mission to reclaim the bathing ritual using only earth-derived botanicals, high-quality plant oils, and rich butters.

What started as a labor of love in a Dallas kitchen has evolved into a dedicated studio where every bar is hand-poured, cured for weeks, and inspected for purity.

Plant-Based Purity

We rely on olive oils, coconut, and shea butters. No synthetic fragrances, no sulfates, and absolutely no parabens.

Small-Batch Ethics

Scaling up shouldn't mean watering down. We maintain small-batch production to ensure every bar meets our clinical standards.

Total Transparency

Like Sliquid, we believe you have the right to know exactly what is in your products. Every ingredient is listed and explained.

"Our goal isn't just to get you clean. It's to give you a moment of quiet, botanical connection in a loud world."
— Shana Adams, Founder
